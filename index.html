About Me

I'm a graduate of Indiana University - Kelley School of Business with a degree in finance along with an exchange program at ESADE Business School and have two years of experience as an analyst at two top ventured backed startups in San Francisco.

As an analyst, I have helped scale both my previous companies abroad to both Spain and Canada, managed strategic initiatives with our clients in the fortune 500, and have assisted in the acquistion of a company sold by WeWork.

My passions revolve around startups & venture capital, philanthropy, and traveling. While I was born and raised in Los Angeles, I have lived in Mexico City, Barcelona, Indiana, and San Francisco.
